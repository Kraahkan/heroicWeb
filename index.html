About Preview

What is Heroic?

Heroic is a storytelling RPG, currently in pre-alpha. The features below are in-progress. You can view my full design goals here.
Classic Fantasy Adventure

Playing Heroic is about telling exciting stories.  Every rule starts and ends with the narrative, pushing you to be creative and immersed.

More Than a Combat Simulator

An epic story contains more than epic fighting. Exploration, interaction, and crafting give classes access to more than combat mechanics

TIGHT RULES

Heroic's rules are focused, eliminating mechanical fluff in favor of clarity and depth. Each mechanic is fast, story-driven, and leads to unique outcomes.

Robust heroes

Traditional classes like the Paladin and modern classes like the Artificer form an impressive roster. Races and Prestige Classes offer additional flexibility.

roll for fun

Fun is prioritized over boring math and unnecessary modifiers.  Heroic's advantage system makes rolling dice easy and intuitive.

Customization

Multiclassing, feats and alternate rules buff out the game for those looking for more. Those who want less will be pleased by the modularity.
