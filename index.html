About Preview

What is Heroic?

Heroic is a storytelling RPG, currently in pre-alpha. The features below are under development and subject to change. You can view my full design goals here.
Fantasy Adventure

Playing Heroic is about telling exciting stories.  Rules start and end with the narrative, putting immersion and creativity on the forefront of the experience.

Robust heroes

Traditional and modern classes come together to form an impressive roster. Races and Prestige Classes add further flexibility to character creation.

More Than a Combat Simulator

An epic story should contain more than epic fighting! Exploration, interaction, and crafting give classes access to more than just combat mechanics.

roll for fun

Fun is prioritized over boring math and unnecessary modifiers.  Heroic's advantage system makes rolling dice easy, intuitive, and meaningful.

TIGHT RULES

Heroic's rules are focused, eliminating mechanical fluff in favor of clarity and depth. Each mechanic is fast, story-driven, and leads to unique outcomes.

Customization

Multiclassing, feats and alternate rules buff out the game for those looking for more. Those who want less will be pleased by the modularity.
